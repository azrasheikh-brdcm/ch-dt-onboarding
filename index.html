Welcome to the Onboarding Checklist

Each Training is Recommended in Order to Take Full Advantage of the VMware Aria Cost Platform



The onboarding checklist provides step-by-step training and configuration guidance in order to help you utilize the platform to its maximum potential. Each module is designed to ensure that you are given the tools to understand the platform and configure it to your liking!


☞ Note: Every training is designed to ensure you can complete the Customer Goals listed. However, if you feel you need additional help, each goal is linked to a help article to ensure you have all the resources you need!
